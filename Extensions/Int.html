  • modulo(_:)

    Performs the modulus (not the remainder) operation on the receiver.

    % is the remainder operator in Swift and not the modulus operator. They return the same results for positive numbers, but they differ for negative numbers.

    -1 % 7 = -1 (remainder)

    -1 mod 7 = 6 (modulus)
