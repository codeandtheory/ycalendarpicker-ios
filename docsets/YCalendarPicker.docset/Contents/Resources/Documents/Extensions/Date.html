Date

extension Date

A date extension with helper function to get all the dates for a selected month. A good choice where we need to show all the dates of a particular month on the calendar view. segment (e.g. getDatesForSelectedMonth).

    Returns [CalendarMonthItem] gives all the dates for selected month dates that need to be shown in current month date calendar (eg. for June 2022, it will give dates from (29th May to 9th July)).
